Welcome to Raheja Lunaris

Raheja Lunaris is an astonishing residential tower in the exceptional township - Raheja District in Vashi NX. It comprises 2 & 3 BHK apartments efficiently designed with the finest interiors, contemporary features, and branded CP fittings. The efficient layout of apartments ensures optimum space utilisation with zero wastage. The township comprises stunning residential, commercial, and retail clusters. Along with the proposed 5-acre District Park, the community offers amenities like a clubhouse, spa, indoor games, swimming pool, kids play area, jogging track, senior citizen corner, gymnasium, banquet hall, and more.

This ambitious locality is equipped with the best social infrastructure and smooth connectivity. Ryan International School, Delhi Public School, DY Patil University, SIES College, New Millennium Multispeciality Hospital, Fortis Hospital, Inorbit Mall, Dmart, and Hotel Vivanta are within a few minutes of reach. The Thane Belapur Road, Juinagar Railway Station, and Sion Panvel Highway offer wider connectivity to Mumbai, Pune, and Thane. The Atal Setu and the upcoming international airport are just a short drive from here. So, embrace a high-standard lifestyle at Raheja Lunaris with captivating indulgences. Read more

About Raheja Universal

Founded in 1980, Raheja Universal is a leading real estate firm in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region (MMR). While the company started by developing luxury residential properties in its early years, it has now expanded to affordable residences, IT parks & commercial towers. With its portfolio boasting more than 50 properties, Raheja Universal has won many awards in recent years.
